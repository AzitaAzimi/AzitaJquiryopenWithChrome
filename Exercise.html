Software Engineering department subjects(5th semester)






((Web Engineering))

This subject is taught by Mr.abdul Rezzaq Hamraz with Miss.Ghazzale Zaheer . This subject helps students to design web pages professionally from view of structure and style. The students by learning this subject should consider these issues to develop a professional products. They must be able to develop web sites with these qualities:

  1. Correctness
  2. Maintainability
  3. Efficiency
  4. Usability
  5. Reusability

To develop a product with high quality the should consider these steps:

Information Gathering:
-what business goals and dreams are,what are their target audience, and how the web can be utilized to help achieve those goals.
Planning:
Using the information gathered from Phase one, it is time to put together a plan for your web site.
Design:
The design stage typically involves moving the information outlined in the planning stage further into reality. It is time to determine the look and feel of your site.
Development:
The developmental stage is the point where the web site itself is created.
Testing and Delivery:
Testing is the process of running a system with the intention of finding errors.
Maintenance:
Maintenance begins when the system is released and continues for the life of the system.
